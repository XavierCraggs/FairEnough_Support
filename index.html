FAQ

Common questions.

Is FairEnough free? ⌄
Yes — the core app is completely free for up to 8 housemates. Chores, Finance, Shopping, and Messenger are all included at no cost. House Pass is an optional upgrade for power features.
Is it available on Android? ⌄
Currently FairEnough is iOS only. Android is on the roadmap — stay tuned.
What is House Fairness? ⌄
House Fairness is a rolling 28-day score that compares each housemate's chore contribution to the house average. It turns a feelings-based argument into a fact-based conversation.
How does bill splitting work? ⌄
Log an expense, choose who paid, and split equally or set custom amounts per person. FairEnough tracks running balances across the house and lets you mark payments as settled.
Is my house data private? ⌄
Yes. Your house data is only accessible to members of your house. We don't sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
How many people can join a house? ⌄
Up to 8 housemates on the free plan. House Pass will support larger households.
