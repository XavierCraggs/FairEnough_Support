Sorted Privacy Policy

Sorted is a sharehouse management app that helps housemates coordinate chores, bills, shopping lists, notifications, and household planning.

Information We Collect

  • Account information such as name, email address, phone number, and profile photo
  • Household data such as chores, bills, shopping items, messages, events, and settings
  • Payment details you choose to save for housemate repayments
  • Device and notification information needed to deliver app notifications
  • Purchase and subscription information related to premium features

How We Use Information

  • To operate the app and provide household coordination features
  • To sync your account and household data across devices
  • To send in-app and push notifications you enable
  • To manage premium access, billing, and support requests
  • To maintain app security, reliability, and performance

Third-Party Services

Sorted may use third-party services to operate the app, including:

  • Firebase for authentication, database, and storage
  • Expo services for app infrastructure and notifications
  • RevenueCat and Apple for subscription and purchase handling

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information is shared only as needed to run the app, process purchases, deliver notifications, comply with legal obligations, or provide support.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the app, maintain records, resolve disputes, and meet legal obligations. Data associated with your account or household may remain in backups or logs for a limited period.

Your Choices

  • You can update profile and household information in the app
  • You can control notification preferences in the app and on your device
  • You can contact support to request help with your account

Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact support@fairenough.app.
